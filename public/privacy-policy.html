🌐 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Short version: We do not store, sell, or share your personal data. Text you translate is sent to a free, third-party translation server. Audio recordings stay on your device and are never uploaded.

1. Who We Are

This app is a phonics learning and real-time translation tool. It is operated independently and is not affiliated with any advertising or data-brokering company.

2. What Data This App Collects

  • Text you submit for translation — sent to a LibreTranslate server (a free, open-source translation API) to perform the translation. This transmission is temporary and subject to LibreTranslate's own privacy policy.
  • Learning progress — stored only in your device's local storage (soles, levels, trophies). Never transmitted.
  • Audio recordings — captured locally for the pronunciation practice feature. Processed entirely on your device and never uploaded anywhere.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking cookies
  • No payment information

4. Third-Party Translation API

Translation requests are sent to one of the following free, open-source LibreTranslate public instances:

  • translate.terraprint.co
  • libretranslate.de
  • lt.vern.cc

As a fallback, requests may also be sent to MyMemory (mymemory.translated.net), a free translation API. These services may log translation requests according to their own policies. We recommend not translating sensitive personal information.

5. Android Overlay & Accessibility Permissions

When installed as an Android app, the following permissions may be requested:

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — allows the floating translation bubble to appear on top of other apps and the home screen. Required for the overlay feature.
  • Accessibility Service (optional) — allows the app to read selected text from other apps so you can tap any word to translate. You control this in Android Settings → Accessibility. The app reads only the text you select; it does not log your screen content.
  • Run on startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — allows the translation overlay service to automatically restart when you reboot your device.
  • Clipboard access — used to auto-detect and translate text you copy from any app. No clipboard content is stored or transmitted except to the translation API for processing.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for educational use including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No account creation is required.

7. Data Retention

Learning progress stored in your browser's localStorage remains until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app. No data is retained on any server controlled by us.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. You can clear all local progress by clearing your browser/app storage at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's repository or contact the developer directly.

This privacy policy applies to the Translation Overlay web app and its Android companion app.
